How to Work From Home and Get Stuff Done

An ever-increasing number of contractors and full-time employees are working from home — or daydreaming about working from home.

Sign up now to receive our daily tips

Create Some Space

Designate a workspace at home for those late nights. This can be anywhere you have access to a flat surface and adequate lighting that allows you to concentrate — a desk, your kitchen table.

Read More

Stay Focused on Work

In addition to your workload, you probably also have a to-do list that includes going grocery shopping, washing the dishes, and doing laundry. And it’s probably making you sweat just thinking about how you’ll manage to get everything done. My advice: Compartmentalize.

Read More

Quit Multitasking

On a similar note, don’t try to fit in the fun stuff on an evening or weekend when you need to be working. If you’re trying to squeeze in writing a blog post during the commercial breaks of American Idol, chances are it won’t be that good, and you’ll end up spending more time on revising it later.
